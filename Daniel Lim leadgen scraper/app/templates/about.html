About Our Tool

Private Equity Lead Generator

Helping private equity firms discover high-potential investment opportunities.

Our lead generation tool is designed specifically for private equity professionals looking to identify potential acquisition targets efficiently. By leveraging Google Maps data and AI-powered analysis, we help you:

  • Discover businesses across various industries and locations
  • Collect comprehensive data including contact information and CEO details
  • Generate AI-powered investment insights for each potential target
  • Export data for integration with your existing workflows

How It Works

Our tool streamlines the business lead generation process for private equity firms:

1. Search

Enter the type of business you're interested in and your target location. Our tool leverages Google Maps API to find matching businesses.

2. Analyze

Select a business to view detailed information. Our system automatically extracts key data including contact information and company leadership.

3. Get Insights

Generate AI-powered investment analyses including growth potential, market position, value creation opportunities, and risk factors.

4. Export

Export selected businesses to CSV or Excel for further analysis or integration with your CRM and due diligence processes.

Privacy & Data Usage

We take data privacy seriously:

  • All business data is collected from publicly available sources
  • Your searches and exports are not stored or shared
  • AI analyses are generated on-demand and not retained

Our tool complies with relevant data protection regulations and Google's terms of service for API usage.
